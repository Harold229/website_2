I am Tankpinou Zoumenou Harold, medical doctor and epidemiologist with a strong interest in biostatistics and data science in health.

I got 2 master degrees, one in epidemiology at university of limoges and the second in biostatistics at sorbonne university. I gained strong knowledge and experience in methodology and biostatistics.

My topics of interest are antibiotic resistance, non communicable disease, infectious disease, mental health.

This year, I will work as a data scientist at the public health department of Assistance des hopitaux publics de paris.

I will be involved in various projects of the department by exploiting health data warehouses.
